IJMEER Journal

International Journal of Multidisciplinary Educational & Ecological Research.

Knowledge for a Better Future

IJMEER is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research at the intersection of education and ecology. As a flagship initiative of Meer Foundation, the journal aims to provide a platform for academics and practitioners to share findings that can drive sustainable development in rural and underserved regions.

Scope of Research

We invite submissions in the following areas:

  • Rural Education Models
  • River Ecology & Conservation
  • Sustainable Agriculture
  • Community Development
  • Vocational Training
  • Indigenous Knowledge

Rigorous Standards

The journal employs a double-blind peer-review process to ensure scientific accuracy and professional integrity. Our editorial board consists of global experts committed to fostering intellectual growth and social impact.
