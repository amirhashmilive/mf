IJMEER Journal

International Journal of Multidisciplinary Emerging Educational Research—a flagship scholarly publishing initiative of Meer Foundation.

Peer Reviewed Open Access ISSN Pending

Advancing Global Knowledge

IJMEER provides a global platform for researchers, academics, and professionals to share transformative ideas in the field of education and multidisciplinary studies. Our journal is dedicated to fostering an ecosystem of rigorous intellectual inquiry and innovation.

Rigorous Academic Standards

The journal employs a rigorous double-blind peer-review system, including plagiarism screening, expert evaluation, and editorial decision-making, with an average publication timeline of 4 to 8 weeks. Each issue is systematically organized with proper bibliographic details and DOI assignment for individual articles, ensuring academic credibility, citation tracking, and indexing compatibility.

The journal maintains quarterly issues, transparent editorial processes, and an open-access model without subscription barriers, supported by a defined Article Processing Charge (APC) structure.
