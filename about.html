Our Story

Established in 2011, working at the intersection of ecology, education, and community.

Mission & Philosophy

Meer Foundation was born from a simple belief: that sustainable development is only possible when communities are at the heart of ecological restoration. For over a decade, we have worked in the forest-fringes and rural heartlands of Chhattisgarh, building self-reliant communities through deep-rooted interventions.

Our philosophy is "Working with people, never for them." By empowering local leadership and integrating scientific research with traditional wisdom, we ensure that our impact is lasting and community-owned.
