Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between rural potential and modern opportunity in Chhattisgarh.

Who We Are

Meer Foundation is a registered non-profit organization (Reg. No. Chhattisgarh Society Registration Act 1973) established in 2011. Based in Dhamtari, we have spent over 14 years working at the grassroots level to empower marginalized communities.

Our approach is rooted in empathy and professional expertise. We don't just impose solutions; we recruit professionals with diverse expertise who work within communities to instill agency and drive long-term social, psychological, and economic change.

Leadership

Founder & President

Amir Hashmi

Founder & President, Meer Foundation

Amir Hashmi is a visionary social entrepreneur and researcher currently pursuing his PhD at Kabindra Tagore University, Jamshedpur. His research focuses on CSR communication and community development in Chhattisgarh and Jharkhand.

He is the creator of the Penta-Alignment Model, a proprietary framework used by Meer Foundation to bridge the gap between corporate social responsibility and genuine societal betterment.
