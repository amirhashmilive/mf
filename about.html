Organization Profile

Meer Foundation is a nonprofit organization established in 2011, dedicated to fostering self-reliance among rural populations in Chhattisgarh. Our approach is holistic, integrating education, health, and conservation into a unified development model.

We believe that the sustainability of a community is inextricably linked to the health of its natural environment. By restoring rivers and soil, we are not just helping nature—we are securing the livelihoods of future generations.
