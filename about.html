Our Story

Founded in 2011, Meer Foundation is a registered nonprofit dedicated to ecological restoration, transformative education, and sustainable livelihoods across Chhattisgarh.

Explore Initiatives Get Involved

Mission & Vision

At Meer Foundation, we believe that development and conservation are two sides of the same coin. Our vision is to create a Chhattisgarh where every citizen lives with dignity, every student has access to global-standard education, and every river flows free from pollution.

For over a decade, we have worked at the grassroots level, combining scientific research with community mobilization to drive systemic change. From our base in Dhamtari, we have expanded our reach across the state, impacting millions of lives through our diverse initiatives.

The Journey So Far

2011

Founding

Meer Foundation is established with a focus on rural education and local livelihood support in Dhamtari.

2016

Launch of Bolti Nadi

Flagship river rejuvenation campaign begins, mapping the health of Chhattisgarh’s major river systems.

2019

Tiranga World Record

Organized a 15km human chain in Raipur to celebrate national unity. Successful Sakri River Walk (90km) completed.

2024

Global Scaling

Launching IJMEER journal and community radio; reaching over 20 million people with water security awareness.
