Yoga Kutumb: For a Healthier Home

Nurturing physical and mental resilience in rural families through certified yoga professionals and holistic wellness practices.

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being

Healthy Families, Strong Nation

Yoga Kutumb is Mehr Foundation’s initiative to bring the profound benefits of Yoga and Ayurveda to the heart of rural Chhattisgarh. We believe that true development begins with the health of the individual and the family.

Our programs are designed and delivered by certified professionals who specialize in traditional Hatha Yoga, Pranayama, and lifestyle management. Since its inception, Yoga Kutumb has reached thousands of villagers, helping them manage chronic conditions and reduce stress.

What We Offer

  • Daily Sessions: Structured yoga classes for different age groups in community halls.
  • Ayurvedic Guidance: Primary wellness consultations using locally available medicinal herbs.
  • Mental Resilience: Meditation and mindfulness workshops for emotional balance.

Community Transformation

Through Yoga Kutumb, we are creating 'Swasthya Mitras' or health friends in every village—volunteers who are trained to lead basic yoga sessions and promote healthy habits within their local neighborhoods.
