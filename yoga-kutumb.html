SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Yoga Kutumb

Ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages through holistic yoga practices.

Holistic Harmony

Yoga Kutumb is an initiative ardently supported by Meer Foundation. Aligned with the ethos of promoting yoga globally, Yoga Kutumb endeavors to integrate the principles of holistic well-being into everyday life. Mirroring the Indian government's commitment to making yoga an intrinsic part of our daily routines, our initiative aspires to create a culture where mind, body, and soul converge in harmony.

Our Goal

Beyond a mere exercise routine, Yoga Kutumb envisions yoga as a universal language promoting a balanced and healthy lifestyle. With certified yoga professionals leading the way, our mission extends to sharing India's ancient wisdom and cultural heritage.

A Global Community

Fostering a global community that embraces the transformative power of yoga. Join us on this holistic journey towards enhanced well-being and unity.
