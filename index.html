WHO WE ARE

A Tradition of Trust & Transformation

Based in Dhamtari, Chhattisgarh, Meer Foundation has been a beacon of hope for rural communities for over a decade. We align our grassroots efforts with the UN Sustainable Development Goals to ensure a resilient and conscious future.

Our work spans across water conservation, quality education, health, and sustainable agriculture, reaching the most remote villages of the state.
