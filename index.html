Sustainable Change for Chhattisgarh

Est. 2011 · Meer Foundation is a registered nonprofit committed to ecological restoration, rural livelihoods, and transformative education across Chhattisgarh.

Our Initiatives

Rooted in community, driven by purpose—our core programs for social and environmental change in Chhattisgarh.

Bolti Nadi

Protecting and restoring the health of Chhattisgarh's river basins. Impacting over 20 million people since 2016 through pollution reduction and flow restoration.

Learn More

Farmours

Securing sustainable livelihoods for smallholder farmers through technical innovation and direct market access to empower rural producers.

Learn More

Prayaas

Delivering quality, digital-integrated education for rural learners. Bridging the digital divide and ensuring equitable access to global standard learning materials.

Learn More

Yoga Kutumb

Nurturing physical and mental resilience. Led by certified professionals to bring holistic well-being to rural families through ancient wisdom and modern practice.

Learn More

Social Heritage Walk

Celebrating the living history of Chhattisgarh. Promoting cultural preservation and archaeological awareness through community-led heritage exploration.

Learn More

Research & Knowledge

Scientific documentation and educational frameworks for long-term sustainability and academic excellence.

Rivers of Chhattisgarh

Scientific research and documentation of Mahanadi, Sheonath, Indravati, and Tandula rivers. Analyzing pollution and volume trends.

Learn More

EduSuTo Courses

Sustainability education through curated learning modules. Empowering the youth with eco-conscious knowledge systems.

Learn More

IJMEER

International Journal of Multidisciplinary Emerging Educational Research. A peer-reviewed platform for scholarly publishing.

Learn More

Emerging Projects

Innovative new platforms to amplify community voices and cultural expression for the digital age.

Community Radio

Upcoming community-owned radio station for rural Chhattisgarh. Bringing information and entertainment to the last mile.

Learn More

Guriya Magazine

Monthly digital magazine amplifying voices of rural girls. Providing a safe space for creative expression and storytelling.
