Tiranga World Record

On **11 August 2019**, Meer Foundation etched its name in history by organizing a massive **15km human chain** in Raipur. This historic event was organized to celebrate national unity and showcased the foundation's capability to mobilize thousands for a collective cause.

The event saw participation from students, professionals, and citizens from all walks of life, stretching across the heart of the capital city. It remains one of the largest civic mobilizations in the history of Chhattisgarh.

Beyond the Numbers

River Rejuvenation

Our Sakri River walk in 2019 led directly to the establishment of a 2-crore STP and desilting works across 59 villages, restoring year-round water access for farmers.

Social Heritage

By highlighting overlooked archaeological sites, we've influenced local conservation policies and brought pride back to rural cultural roots.

Digital Literacy

Thousands of rural students who had never seen a computer are now learning coding and digital skills, breaking the cycle of rural-urban educational inequality.
