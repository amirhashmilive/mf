Guriya Magazine

A monthly digital platform amplifying the dreams, struggles, and creativity of rural girls in Chhattisgarh. Empowering through expression.

Monthly Digital Edition SDG 5: Gender Equality

Empowering Through Storytelling

Guriya is Meer Foundation's dedicated space for rural girls to explore their creative potential. We believe that behind every pair of eyes in our villages is a unique story of resilience and hope. Guriya Magazine provides the language and the platform for these stories to be heard.

Every month, we curate poems, illustrations, essays, and photo-stories submitted by girls from across our operational blocks. By documenting their lived experiences, we are not only preserving local culture but also building the confidence of young women leaders.

Magazine Content Units

Kavita-Kunj

Traditional and modern poetry reflecting on nature and life.

Sapno Ki Udaan

Career-oriented articles and interviews with successful rural women.

Kahani-varta

Folk tales and oral histories narrated by the younger generation.

Humar Kala

Showcase of traditional arts and digital crafts by rural girls.
