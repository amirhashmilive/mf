Farmours: Secure Livelihoods

Empowering smallholder farmers through technical innovation, sustainable practices, and direct market integration to double rural incomes.

SDG 1: No Poverty SDG 8: Decent Work

The Backbone of Rural Economy

Farmours is a flagship livelihood initiative by Meer Foundation, specifically designed to address the systemic challenges faced by small and marginal farmers in Chhattisgarh. In a landscape dominated by unpredictable weather and fluctuating market prices, Farmours acts as a shield for the rural producer.

Our approach is rooted in **Climate-Smart Agriculture**. We bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern technology, providing farmers with soil health cards, high-yield organic seeds, and localized weather forecasting through mobile alerts.

Strategic Interventions

Organic Certification

Helping farmers transition to organic farming and obtain certifications for premium market pricing.

Direct Market Linkages

Eliminating middlemen by connecting Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) directly to urban retail chains.

Micro-Irrigation

Promoting drip and sprinkler systems to optimize water usage in drought-prone blocks.

Farm Mechanization

Providing access to tool banks where small farmers can rent modern machinery at subsidized rates.

Dignity for the Producer

Beyond income, Farmours is about dignity. We organize annual 'Kisan Sammelans' where farmers are recognized for their innovations and leadership. By turning farming into a viable, proud profession, we are stemming the tide of distress migration from Chhattisgarh’s villages to big cities.
