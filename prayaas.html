SDG 4: Quality Education

Prayaas

Reshaping the educational landscape for rural students across Chhattisgarh.

Educational Equity

Prayaas is a transformative initiative spearheaded by Meer Foundation. This groundbreaking endeavor is dedicated to reshaping the educational landscape in rural Chhattisgarh, recognizing the vast potential of education and offering high-quality experiences tailored for the region's most underserved students.

Bridging the Gap

The initiative bridges the gap between traditional learning and modern educational methodologies. We emphasize academic excellence while advocating for universal access to the best educational resources, ensuring that no student is left behind in the evolving digital age.
