Prayaas: Quality Rural Education

Bridging the digital divide and ensuring equitable access to global-standard learning materials for underserved students in Chhattisgarh.

SDG 4: Quality Education

Our Mission for Rural Minds

Prayaas is Meer Foundationâ€™s dedicated initiative to deliver high-quality, digital-integrated education to the remotest corners of Chhattisgarh. We believe that a child in a forest village deserves the same access to knowledge as a child in a metropolitan city.

Through our network of community learning centers and mobile education units, we provide digital literacy, curriculum support, and vocational training that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Key Focus Areas

  • Digital Access: Setting up computer labs and providing internet-enabled tablets to rural learners.
  • Teacher Training: Empowering local educators with modern pedagogical techniques and digital tools.
  • Literacy & Numercy: Specialized focus on foundational skills in both regional and national languages.
