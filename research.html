Rivers of Chhattisgarh

A comprehensive research project mapping the health of tributaries across Chhattisgarh. We document water quality, riparian biodiversity, and the socio-economic impact of river health on rural populations.

  • • Periodic Water Quality Index (WQI) reports
  • • Biodiversity mapping of river banks

EduSuTo Courses

Education for Sustainable Transformation (EduSuTo). We develop specialized training modules for rural youth and community leaders on sustainable living, eco-entrepreneurship, and water management.

  • • Certification programs for youth
  • • Skill development workshops

IJMEER

The International Journal of Meer Education, Environment & Research (IJMEER) is our platform for academic publications. We provide a space for researchers to publish peer-reviewed papers on rural development and conservation.
