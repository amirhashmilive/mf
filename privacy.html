Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your personal data.

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you donate, volunteer, or contact us through our website, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address. We also collect transaction data for donations per regulatory requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process donations, send receipts, provide updates on our initiatives, and respond to your inquiries. We do not sell or trade your data with third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. All online donations are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways (Razorpay).

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at meercare@live.com.
