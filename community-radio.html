Community Radio

Establishing a rural voice for information, climate awareness, and folk culture.

The Last Mile Voice

Our upcoming community radio initiative aims to bridge the information gap in remote parts of Chhattisgarh. By broadcasting in vernacular languages, we will provide real-time updates on weather, agricultural advice, health alerts, and educational content.

This station will also serve as a repository for the region's rich oral traditions, folk music, and linguistic heritage.

Key Focus Areas

  • Weather & Climate: Localized alerts for farmers.
  • Health & Hygiene: Awareness programs on nutrition and safety.
  • Culture: Folk songs and traditional storytelling.
  • Education: Supplementary lessons for rural students.
