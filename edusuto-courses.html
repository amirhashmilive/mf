EduSuTo Courses

Education for Sustainable Tomorrow—curated learning modules that empower rural youth with the knowledge to build eco-conscious communities.

Environmental Literacy Skill Development

Learning for a Green Future

EduSuTo is Meer Foundation's specialized knowledge platform. We bridge the gap between academic theory and practical environmental stewardship. Our courses are designed for the local context of Chhattisgarh, focusing on water conservation, sustainable agriculture, and renewable energy.

Water Literacy

Understanding hydrological cycles, groundwater recharge, and the importance of bankside ecosystems.

Permaculture

Practical modules on designing farm ecosystems that work with nature rather than against it.

Digital Ecology

Using GIS and data tools to monitor local environmental health and report pollution.

Social Leadership

Governance and community mobilization strategies for local environmental committees.

Course Highlights

Our courses are available in both Hindi and Chhattisgarhi to ensure maximum reach.
